How does reading with Learning Ally benefit Carlos?

When students like Carlos read with Learning Ally, they develop skills critical for learning and lifelong success. Research has shown that extensive reading - including Learning Ally audiobooks - increases general knowledge, vocabulary, spelling, verbal fluency, and comprehension.
